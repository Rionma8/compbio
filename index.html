Welcome!

The CompBio@TrEE is the computational biology group of the TrEE team (TIMC lab) in Grenoble. Our research is dedicated to the development of methods and tools to analyse, interpret, and decipher biological data and processes, with a focus on the evolution of prokaryotes.

We are composed of experts in biophysics, comparative genomics and machine learning.

Research interest

  • Biological questions

    • Organization of genomes
    • Origins and evolution of functions
    • Regulation of gene expression

  • Methodological development

    • Biophysics of DNA
    • Comparative genomics
    • Machine learning in high dimensions
